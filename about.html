EST. 2018 / MISSION

Bridging the gap between technical precision and human warmth.

At Coco Drafting, we believe that every blueprint is a promise of a future space. Our mission is to provide architects, developers, and homeowners with meticulous drafting services that blend the rigidity of structural engineering with the fluidity of organic living.

Our Legacy

Founded in a small coastal studio, Coco Drafting was born from the realization that technical drawings often lacked the "soul" of the projects they represented.

Starting with just three founders and a single drafting table, we have grown into a multi-disciplinary boutique firm serving over 500 successful residential and commercial developments.
