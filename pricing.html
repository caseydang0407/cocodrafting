Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know which tier is right for me?

If you're in the early stages, the Consultation is best. For renovations or straightforward additions, Drafting Pro covers all basics. New builds typically require Full Delivery.

Are structural engineering fees included?

No, architectural drafting focuses on design and layout. We can recommend and coordinate with structural engineers for an additional project management fee.

What is the typical turnaround time?

Consultations are immediate. Drafting Pro sets take 2-4 weeks, while Full Delivery projects range from 8-12 weeks depending on complexity.

Do you handle the permit process?

In the Full Delivery tier, we manage the submission and liaison with city planning. For other tiers, we provide the drawings you need to submit yourself.
